# Business Central Water Billing System

## Overview

A custom **Dynamics 365 Business Central Water Billing System** built to automate meter reading, water usage calculation, monthly invoicing, SMS notifications, payments, and reporting for water supply businesses.
<img width="950" height="338" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a26441d9-b717-4ff6-89f3-f5632c11f050" />

## Key Features

* Customer meter management
* Weekly meter reading entry
* Automatic water usage calculation
* Meter ledger entries
* Monthly invoice generation
* Automatic sales invoice posting
* Duplicate billing prevention
* SMS notifications to customers
* Payment integration
* Water usage reports
* Custom Role Center dashboard

## Process Flow

Meter Reading
   ↓
Usage Calculation
   ↓
Meter Ledger Entry
   ↓
Monthly Billing
   ↓
Sales Invoice Posted
   ↓
SMS Sent to Customer

## Integrations

The system supports integration with:

* SMS providers for customer notifications
* Mobile money or bank payment platforms
* Business Central financial management
* External systems through APIs
